Our Mission

Since Fall of 2019, the sole purpose of Kids Read has been to provide under-served children in Salt Lake Title I schools and Uganda with educational support. We have done this with a ‘Little Learners Library’ placed in each of our locations. Children are encouraged to take a book, read it and return it for another. In November of 2021 we opened Kids Read Salt Lake Valley. Local Ambassadors and Tutors provide the help they need to learn to Read & Succeed. In August, 2021 we added the Feed 2 Read Nutrition Program to feed the minds and bodies of children in Uganda with the support of the Usana Foundation.
